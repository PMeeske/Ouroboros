# Challenges in AI Development

## Technical Challenges
- Data quality and quantity requirements
- Computational costs for training large models
- Model interpretability and explainability
- Handling edge cases and adversarial inputs

## Ethical Challenges
- Bias amplification from training data
- Privacy concerns with data collection
- Deepfakes and misinformation
- Autonomous weapons concerns

## Economic Challenges
- Job displacement and workforce transition
- Concentration of AI capabilities in few companies
- Digital divide between AI-rich and AI-poor regions
