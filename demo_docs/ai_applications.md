# AI Applications in 2025

## Healthcare
AI is revolutionizing diagnostics, drug discovery, and personalized medicine. Machine learning models can detect cancer earlier than human doctors.

## Finance
Algorithmic trading, fraud detection, and credit scoring rely heavily on AI. Risk assessment has become more accurate.

## Transportation
Autonomous vehicles are becoming mainstream. AI optimizes traffic flow in smart cities.

## Education
Personalized learning paths, automated grading, and intelligent tutoring systems are transforming education.
